Oryx Computer Systems (W/A) Limited also known as "Oryx Africa"​ is a business consultancy company. Since inception in 2004, Oryx has garnered the trust of the market leaders and is constantly unlocking world class best practices to companies. Its unparalleled approach has witnessed success stories from start-ups to growing and sophisticated ones; from wholesale distribution, high-end retail items, and professional services to complex manufacturing, engineering and construction operations. Our expertise cuts across several industries - Engineering Construction and operations, Oil and Gas, Logistics, Food and Beverages, Advertisement, Wholesale distribution, Manufacturing, Pharmaceuticals and more. We are proud to be partners in progress with several world class companies- SAP, Sage and Microsoft.
